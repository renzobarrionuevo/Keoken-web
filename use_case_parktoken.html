ParkToken

Fictional case of the creation of a token related to amusement parks.

amusement park

A worldwide amusement park company creates a token on the Keoken platform called ParkToken (PT). The purpose of the token is to avoid the use of cash and currency exchanges in the amusement park. This facilitates the park management and avoids employees managing cash.

Let's see a practical example:

  • A family visits an amusement park. At arrival they buy 1,000 PTs using tools available in the park.
  • They pay 200 PTs for the park tickets.
  • After visiting some attractions they decide to get lunch and use the KeoKen wallet to pay 150 PTs to buy hotdogs and another 50 PTs to buy sodas.
  • To avoid doing long queues, they decide to pay to use the express line at two attractions for 25 PTs per attraction.
  • Before leaving, they go to the gift shop and buy key rings and a teddy bear for the girl for a total of 250 PTs.
  • After leaving the park they still have 300 PTs remaining which can be used in a future park visit, exchanged through the KeoKen wallet with another user or exchanged for 0.3 Keos to be used in other services.
