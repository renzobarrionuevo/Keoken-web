MoveToken

Fictional case of the creation of a token related to passenger transport.

Mobility Token

MoveToken is created by a group of companies related to transport. The group is integrated by an airline, an urban transport company and a fuel company.

An fuel company creates a token called MoveToken (MT). Acquire a total of 1 Million MT, valued at 10,000 Keo (100 MT = 1 Keo).

Let’s see MoveToken in action:

  • An airline user buys a plane ticket using the company’s platform and because of that the company rewards him 1 MT for every 100 Miles in the flight.
  • The user takes the 1,000 Miles flight, obtaining 10 MT.
  • At arrival he decides to take urban transport. The company chosen allows him to pay with MTs.
  • With his 10 MT he pays for the 50% of the fare and arrives at destiny.
  • The driver of the vehicle taken by the user can reuse the MTs in a gas station owned by a fuel company that accepts MT.
  • The fuel company accepts MTs in exchange for discounts in fuel loads and the purchase of car maintenance products.
  • The driver uses 5 MTs to refill the tank of his car with a 10% discount.
  • The driver exchanges the remaining 5 MTs for 0.05 Keos using the Keoken trader with the intention of exchange the Keo for other tokens in the platform.
