Keoken

An open protocol for extending blockchain functionality

What is Keoken?

Keoken is a second Layer solution that involves a digital asset accounting and secure smart contracts platform. Focused on real world use cases, approaching them in an efficient and simple way, it intends to cover most of the situations that involve transfers of value between parties and the business rules required to run most common activities.

Learn more

What we offer?

Keoken fills the gap between reliable cryptocurrencies protocols and less reliable smart contracts platforms. We offer you the possibility to create your own asset with its own set of rules defined by you on a platform that you control with your vote.

Learn more

Keoken Wallet

The Keoken wallet is a service that allows users to keep track of all the tokens they have on the Keoken protocol. It also allows the operation with the Keoken protocol creating and sending tokens to other Bitcoin Cash addresses.

Learn more

Blockchain second layer platform

Decentralized technologies promise to facilitate our lives but they are struggling with escalating and accommodating to our necessities. This is our platform's main reason of existence.

Simplicity

Our platform offers a small set of simple transaction formats that covers the mayority of real life commercial exchanges. Smart Contracts can be constructed in cases where more complexity is needed.

Efficiency

Second layers offer a more efficient way of using the blockchain. Aditionally our protocol interpreter guarantees the optimization of computing resources.

Flexibility

Keoken is designed to dynamically adapt to changing market conditions by including rules and processes to mold any aspect of the Keoken ecosystem. We promote and encourage parties to freely define their own exchange rules.

Security

Our security model relies on the strength of Bitcoin Cash's POW, building a multi-layer dynamic governance system on top of it and giving asset owners control over the rules that affect their assets.
