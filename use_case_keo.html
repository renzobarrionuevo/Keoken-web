Keo Use Cases

The purpose of the following use cases is to demonstrate the reach of the platform. We will show with practical examples some of the diverse ways Keos can be used, the different tokens created through the platform and the interactions between them.

  • An fuel company creates a token called MoveToken (MT). Acquires a total of 1Million MTs, valued at 10,000 Keos (100 MT = 1 Keo).
  • A second amusement park company creates a token and calls it ParkToken (PT). Acquires a total of 10 Million PTs, valued at 10,000 Keos (1000 PT = 1 Keo).
  • A user goes to a gas station. For the load of a full tank the company rewards him 10 MTs.
  • Those 10 MTs are transferred to the user’s personal wallet using a QR code reader.
  • The user goes to an amusement park and discovers it is part of the KeoKen Network. Using the available trader in his wallet he exchanges his 10 MT for 0.1 Keos and then acquires 100 PT with them.
  • In the park he uses the KeoKen wallet and his PTs to get a discount in the ticket. He also acquires more PTs to spend them inside the park.
  • When he leaves the park, he still has a remainder of 300 PTs and decides to exchange them back for 0,3 Keos to use in other services included in the Keoken Network.
